Learning Reflection

My partner and I are looking at either buying or setting up a kiwifruit orchard. I’d like to be able use web development and data science To help track changes is the different parts of the orchard. Incorporating sensors that are available today and research in to soil science to incorporate regenerative ag ideas and concepts into an orchard environment.

Completing this course will also allow me to have an income while working towards my goals.

I used to quite a social person, working in Human Resources, talking to people came easy to me. I could start a conversation with anyone. Over that last few years due to a variety of circumstances I became quite isolated, I feel I’ve lost that ease I used to have dealing with and forming connections with people. I would like get my people skills back, network and learn to work as part of a team again.

As I have mentioned in a previous post I love learning, but I have to be careful I don’t get lost “down the rabbit hole” when exploring and researching a topic. I am a prolific researcher, and I love nothing more than a good intellectual debate.

I have been out structured employment for a number of years, helping my partner assisting my partner working on property. I no longer have a routine to my days. I need to establish some habits in my day, get up earlier (which is always difficult as I am no morning person!) and create a plan for what I want to achieve that day. I am hoping that by the time bootcamp starts the room I intend to work from is finished and I’ll have an area free of distractions. I’ve been using a Pomodoro planning app. So far it has worked well to remind me to take a break, but also plan my time for each task. I feel this part of the course, planning my time, actually sticking to it will be vital to getting me through the course work on time.

When I hit a roadblock during this course I will turn to my facilitator, the slack channel (foundations) or discord(boot camp) in order to get help from fellow students and teachers.

I expect the team at Dev Academy to not solve a problem for me but to lead me towards it. Guiding my learning to areas I need to work on. I hope to establish a good relationship with the team at Dev and also with my Cohort. I feel that with the technology we have today this is possible even through remote learning.

The only commitments I have during the day right now is making sure take my dogs outside a few times during the day.
